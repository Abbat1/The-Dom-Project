Chrome Hearts

Chrome Hearts. A rockstar/biker like brand that implemented gothic sterling silver & heavy leather garments. As the brand arose in a backyard in 1988 by Richard Stark and John Bowman, they hit the market striking celebs, underground artists & mainstream artists. It's a staple peice for those who understand the background behind not only the music, but one's way of living.

Yohji Yamamoto

Some may call him the king of Avant Garde.Yamamoto is highly known for his intentional, directional cuts and mastering tailored looks creating multiple illusions within one piece. His draprey aesthetic leave room for comfort and creativity with his pieces. His influences in fashion come and go with trends to this day. We Thank you Yohji.
